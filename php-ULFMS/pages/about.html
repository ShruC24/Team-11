About Us

PES University, situated in Bangalore, India, is a prominent private educational institution celebrated for its comprehensive range of academic programs spanning engineering, management, and design. Renowned for its modern campus equipped with cutting-edge facilities, the university places a strong emphasis on fostering research initiatives and industry collaborations. Beyond academics, PES University nurtures a vibrant student life through diverse extracurricular activities and cultural events. For the latest and detailed information on programs, admission criteria, and campus life, it's advisable to visit the official website or directly contact the university.
